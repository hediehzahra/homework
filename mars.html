MARS

Mars is a cold desertworld.It is half the size of Earth. Mars is sometimes called the Red Planet. It's red because of rusty iron in the ground.
Along with Venus, Earth, and Mars, Mercury is one of the rocky planets. It has a solid surface that is covered with craters.
It has no atmosphere, and it doesn’t have any moons. Mercury likes to keep things simple.
